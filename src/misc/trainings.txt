Many firms have similar features in their training & certification offerings, common themes across firms are,

Leadership & soft skills training: Especially for those moving into managerial roles, or as part of “high potential” programs.

Functional / technical upskilling: E.g. data & analytics, ESG / sustainability, digital transformation, auditing, tax, Salesforce / CRM tools.

Certification support: Helping employees obtain external credentials (e.g. CPA, professional accounting / auditing / risk / technology‑related credentials). Often reimbursement or bonuses tied.

Blended delivery: In‑person, virtual classroom, self‑paced e‑learning, on‑the‑job training.

Internal academies / bootcamps: For candidates, early career hires, or when entering new areas of practice.

Networks / peer learning: Many programs include peer cohorts, mentorship, case studies, etc
